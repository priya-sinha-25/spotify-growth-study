# Part 2 Primary Research — Google Form (12 Questions)

**Segment:** Premium subscribers whose Discover Weekly has stopped surfacing new music

**Create at:** [https://forms.google.com](https://forms.google.com) → Blank form

**Settings:** Limit to 1 response → On · Quiz → Off · Collect emails → Off

---

## Form title

```
Is Your Discover Weekly Stuck on Repeat? 🎧
```

## Form description

```
Every Monday, millions of us open Discover Weekly hoping for that one song that changes everything.

Sometimes it delivers. Sometimes it's the same artists. Again. And again.

We're building something better for listeners who are tired of déjà vu in their weekly playlist. This 3-minute survey is your chance to tell us what discovery should feel like — not what an algorithm thinks it should be.

No interviews. No sales pitch. Just honest answers from people who actually use Discover Weekly.

All responses are anonymous. Hit submit and go back to your playlists. 🎵
```

---

## Q1. Do you have Spotify Premium?
- **Type:** Multiple choice · **Required**
- Yes, I pay for Premium
- I had Premium in the last 6 months
- No, free only

## Q2. How often do you use Discover Weekly?
- **Type:** Multiple choice · **Required**
- Every week — it's part of my routine
- 2–3 times per month
- Rarely or never

## Q3. Has Discover Weekly recently recommended songs you already heard, liked, or saved?
- **Type:** Multiple choice · **Required**
- Yes, often — feels like a rerun
- Yes, sometimes
- Rarely or never — it's usually fresh

## Q4. Compared to 1–2 years ago, Discover Weekly feels:
- **Type:** Linear scale · **Required**
- **1** = Much more repetitive · **3** = About the same · **5** = More surprising

## Q5. Which of these have you felt with Discover Weekly or Spotify recommendations? (Select all that apply)
- **Type:** Checkboxes · **Required**
- Same artists showing up week after week
- Songs already in my library appearing in DW
- Recommendations feel boring or predictable
- Stuck in one genre or mood
- Same songs across DW, Daily Mix, and Radio
- None of these — I'm happy with discovery

## Q6. When Discover Weekly lets you down, what do you usually do?
- **Type:** Multiple choice · **Required**
- Go back to my saved playlists
- Try Daily Mix, Release Radar, or Radio instead
- Switch to another app (Apple Music, YouTube Music, etc.)
- Hunt for recommendations on Reddit, friends, or social
- Listen less on Spotify or think about cancelling Premium

## Q7. Finish this sentence: "What frustrates me most about Spotify recommendations is ___"
- **Type:** Short answer · **Required**

## Q8. When you're in the mood for something new, what are you actually looking for?
- **Type:** Multiple choice · **Required**
- Break out of songs I've been overplaying
- New artists that still feel like "me"
- A completely different genre or mood
- A few surprises without much effort
- Honestly, I mostly want background music

## Q9. A "perfect" Discover Weekly would give you: (Pick up to 2)
- **Type:** Checkboxes · **Required**
- Mostly songs I've never heard before
- Artists outside my usual rotation
- A short note on why each song was picked
- A reset button when the playlist feels stale
- A smart mix of familiar and new

## Q10. Imagine Spotify offered an AI helper for when DW feels stale. Which response would you want?
- **Type:** Multiple choice · **Required**
- 30 new tracks — just give me a playlist
- 3 tracks with a clear "why this might work for you"
- A quick chat about my mood, then suggestions
- Chat first, then a few explained picks

## Q11. What would make you actually trust an AI music suggestion? (Select all that apply)
- **Type:** Checkboxes · **Required**
- It explains why the song fits me
- It skips songs already in my library
- It nudges me slightly outside my comfort zone
- It feels noticeably different from my last few Discover Weeklies
- I can give feedback and it adjusts
- Nothing — I wouldn't trust it

## Q12. If Spotify launched this AI discovery helper tomorrow, how likely would you use it?
- **Type:** Linear scale · **Required**
- **1** = Definitely not · **3** = Maybe · **5** = Definitely yes, every week
